Discover the Heart of Botswana at Bongs Haven!

Our Story

Imagine a place where the vibrant rhythm of Botswana’s culture comes alive on your plate. That’s exactly what we aim to deliver at Bongs Haven. Founded by passionate food lovers, our journey began with a simple idea: to share the authentic flavors of Botswana with food enthusiasts far and wide. And boy, have we been on a delicious adventure!

What Makes Us Special

From the fiery Seswaa (slow-cooked beef) to the sweet and tangy Morogo (wild spinach), every dish tells a story of tradition, community, and celebration. Our secret? We use family recipes that have been handed down through generations, combined with fresh ingredients sourced from local farmers. It’s food that not only satisfies your taste buds but also warms your soul.

Join Our Food Adventure

Whether you’re a seasoned lover of Botswana cuisine or a curious newcomer, we promise an experience packed with bold flavors and unforgettable moments. Our friendly staff will guide you through our menu, sharing stories behind each dish. Plus, our lively atmosphere, inspired by Botswana’s vibrant culture, makes every visit feel like a celebration.

Did You Know?

Botswana is famous for its biltong, a delicious dried meat snack loved worldwide!
